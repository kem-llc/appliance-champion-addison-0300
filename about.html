Our story

Your Trusted Appliance Repair Experts in Addison

Our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable appliance repair to every Addison homeowner and business.

Appliance Champion was founded with a simple goal: to offer appliance repair that's honest, transparent, and high-quality. Our founder, a veteran technician, saw too many customers overcharged for shoddy work. So we built a company that puts the customer first — with upfront pricing, same-day service, and a real warranty.

Today, we serve the entire Addison area from our Dooley Road location. Our team of certified technicians has repaired thousands of appliances, from refrigerators to dishwashers. We're proud to be a local business that neighbors trust.
